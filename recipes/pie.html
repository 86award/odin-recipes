Squirrel Pot Pie

As the name suggests, it's not fancy, it's a squirrel cooked in a pot, with some 'pastry'.

  • 1 x dead squirrel, preferably (it's easier that way).
  • 1 x metal container. A used can is ideal. Don't throwaway anything already in the can, it can only add to the flavour.
  • Honestly, you're not going to be making pastry in the apocalypse so maybe an irradiated leaf will do.
  1. Put the dead squirrel in the pot. Cover with whatever you've managed to find to act as pastry.
  2. Heat to a very hot temperature to cook the irradiated meat thoroughly.
  3. Enjoy. Yummy!
